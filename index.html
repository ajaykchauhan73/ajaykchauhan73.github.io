About me

A backend engineer who cares about clean architecture, reliability, and shipping things that hold up under real-world load.

I'm a Backend Software Engineer with 2+ years of experience designing and operating high-traffic Java microservices at Octro Inc., a leading online gaming platform serving millions of active users.

My work spans the full backend lifecycle — from RESTful API design and payment-gateway integration to OTP-based authentication and database performance tuning. I build stateless, scalable services backed by RabbitMQ and ZooKeeper, and I'm fluent in Core Java, Spring Boot, and Hibernate/JPA — thriving in fast-moving agile teams where reliability is non-negotiable.

Beyond the day job, I love building things end-to-end — a mining-compliance platform on AWS, a full-stack dairy automation system, and a multi-threaded algorithmic trading bot. I learn fastest by shipping.
